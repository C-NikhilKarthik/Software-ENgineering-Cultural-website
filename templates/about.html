About Us

We are a community-driven initiative that aims to provide a first-of-its-kind, multi-disciplinary space for the Arts in cities across India. Our goal is to celebrate the vibrance of India's heritage and provide a platform for emerging talent to showcase their work.

We believe that the Arts have the power to bring communities together and enrich our lives in ways that nothing else can. Our dynamic programming includes epic theatricals, regional theatre, music, dance, spoken word, and much more.

We work closely with Art communities through collaborations, aggregators,and sponsors to curate unique experiences that are accessible to all. Join us on our journey to discover, celebrate and share the richness of India's cultural heritage.
